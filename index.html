Welcome to UQSay

UQSay is a series of seminars on the broad area of Uncertainty Quantification (UQ), Design and Analysis of Computer Experiments (DACE), and related topics.

It is organized by a merry gathering of people from:

  • L2S (Univ. Paris-Saclay, CentraleSupélec, CNRS),
  • MSSMAT (Univ. Paris-Saclay, CentraleSupélec, CNRS),
  • EDF R&D,
  • LMT (Univ. Paris-Saclay, ENS Paris-Saclay, CNRS),
  • DOTA (Univ. Paris-Saclay, ONERA),
  • LISN (Univ. Paris-Saclay, CNRS),
  • LMO (Univ. Paris-Saclay, CNRS)
  • and MIA-Paris (Univ. Paris-Saclay, AgroParisTech, INRAE)
